MAP

Some Heatmaps About Chinese Meteorology

We have created heatmaps for the provinces across China, depicting temperature, precipitation, wind speed, sunshine duration, and AQI index over the months. You can explore these visualizations by selecting different criteria.





More

Further Analysis of the Data

By drawing heat maps for each province, we can only make rough observations. We further created line charts, radar charts, and parallel coordinate plots, among other visualizations, for a more in-depth analysis of the data.

Determinie the Theme and Team Members

At the beginning, we decided that each person would create a separate webpage, with individual themes focusing on: temperature, precipitation, wind direction and speed, sunshine duration, and air pollution.
